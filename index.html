"Finding Nemo" is an animated movie that tells the story of a cautious clownfish (Marlin) who sets out on a risky quest across the ocean to locate his son Nemo, who has been taken prisoner by a diver.
Marlin travels with Dory, a friendly but forgetful blue tang, and they meet a variety of aquatic animals and roadblocks along the way.

The main message of Finding Nemo is about overcoming fear, accepting help from others, learning from mistakes and the importance of friendship

The Lion King 1994
  • Animation
  • Adventure
  • Action
  • Children's Film

"The Lion King" is an animated movie that follows the adventures of the young lion (Simba), the heir of his father (Mufasa).
Simba's wicked uncle (Scar), plots to usurp Mufasa's throne by luring father and son into a stampede of wildebeests.
But Simba escapes, and only Mufasa is killed.
Simba returns as an adult to take back his homeland from Scar with the help of his friends (Timon) and (Pumbaa).

The main message of The Lion King is about is the ability to forgive ourselves for the past, the mistakes we have made and the things we can't change, and look to the future with pride at how far we've come on our journey, and how much more there is to learn and experience in the world around us.
